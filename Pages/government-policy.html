Government & Legal Policy

Samaze complies with applicable laws and regulations to ensure legal and ethical operation of the platform.

Data Protection

  • User data is handled according to applicable data protection laws.
  • Personal information is stored securely and not shared without consent.
  • Users have the right to request deletion of their personal data.

Legal Compliance

  • The platform follows national cyber laws and digital regulations.
  • Illegal activities such as fraud, hacking, or distribution of illegal content are prohibited.
  • Authorities may be notified if serious violations occur.

User Responsibility

  • Users must ensure their content complies with local laws.
  • False information or harmful misinformation may be removed.
  • Violation of laws may result in legal action.

These policies help ensure the platform operates within the legal framework and protects the rights of all users.
