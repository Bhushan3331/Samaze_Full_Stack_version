Company Policy

Welcome to Samaze. These policies ensure a safe, respectful, and secure platform for all users.

Community Guidelines

  • Users must treat others with respect and avoid harassment or hate speech.
  • Posting harmful, illegal, or abusive content is strictly prohibited.
  • Spam, fake engagement, or misleading content is not allowed.
  • Users must not impersonate other individuals or organizations.

Content Policy

  • Users are responsible for the content they post.
  • Copyrighted material must not be shared without permission.
  • Graphic violence or adult content may be removed by moderators.

Account Safety

  • Users must keep their account credentials secure.
  • Accounts involved in suspicious activities may be temporarily restricted.
  • Repeated violations may result in account suspension.

By using Samaze, you agree to follow these policies and help maintain a positive community.
